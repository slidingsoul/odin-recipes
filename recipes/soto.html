Soto

Description

Soto is an Indonesian soup composed of yellow broth, meat or chicken, and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 700 grams of chicken
  • 2.5 L of water
  • A teaspoon of salt
  • A teaspoon of sugar
  • Two tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Two lemongrasses
  • 5 lime leaves
  • 2 cm of pounded galangal
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil for sauteing
  • 300 mL cooking oil for frying

Ground Spices

  • 12 onions
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cm of ginger
  • 3cm of baked turmeric
  • 4 candlenuts
  • A tablespoon of fried garlic
  • A tablespoon of fried onion

Steps

  1. Heat up the water until boiled
  2. Boil the chicken over low heat until soft and drain it
  3. Fry the chicken until golden brown
  4. Make a broth using water for boiling chicken until the broth is boiling
  5. Heat up cooking oil and sautee ground spices, lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves until fragrant
  6. Pour stir-fried spices, salt, sugar, and soy sauce to the broth
  7. Cook until boiled
