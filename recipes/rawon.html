Rawon

Description

Rawon is an Indonesian food, a meat soup. Its uniqueness lies in the dark broth. Rawon originated in Ponorogo, Jawa Timur

Ingredients

Main Spices

  • 2cm of pounded galangal
  • 5 lime leaves
  • 2 bashed lemongrass
  • Pepper Powder to taste
  • Palm Sugar to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil

Ground Spices

  • 5 Keluak Fruit Flesh
  • 5 Peeled Ginger
  • 1 Peeled Aromatic Ginger
  • 1 Peeled Turmeric
  • 10 Peeled Onion Clove
  • 5 Peeled Garlic Clove
  • 5 Candlenut
  • Half teaspoon of Coriander
  • Half teaspoon of Caraway
